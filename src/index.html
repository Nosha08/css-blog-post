Evan's Tragedy

Evan Yegorov had been a simple man for most of his life. He enjoyed playing basketball and hanging out with his friends, but most importantly, was spending time with his beloved anime girlfriend. To Evan, she was precious and the sole person that could keep him happy. However, on September 14th at 11:23 AM, crisis struck. Evan had been caught making out with her on the library computer. Both Evan's mother and the school administration took away his love, hope, dreams, and true source of happiness, effictively marking the end of his dream-like relationship with his beloved anime girlfriend.
